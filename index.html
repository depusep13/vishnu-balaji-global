About Vishnu Balaji Global

Vishnu Balaji Global Imports & Exports is a leading Indian mercantile export corporation specializing in sourcing, warehousing, processing, and exporting premium-grade agricultural grains, fresh produce, spices, pulses, and dry fruits.

Backed by direct procurement channels from growers and modern cold-chain infrastructures, we ensure consistent physical quality, standardized sizing, low moisture thresholds, and export-compliant certifications. We handle end-to-end export requirements, facilitating private labeling, customized pack sizes, phytosanitary clearance, and custom port logistics.
